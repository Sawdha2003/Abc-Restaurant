Our Story

At ABC Restaurant, we're proud to share our story of passion, dedication, and delicious food. Established in 2000, our main branch in Colombo has been the hub of our culinary journey, with multiple branches spread across the island. Over the years, we've been committed to serving a diverse range of cuisines that cater to every palate. From traditional favorites to innovative twists, our menu is designed to satisfy the cravings of our customers. With a focus on quality, freshness, and flavor, we strive to provide an exceptional dining experience that leaves a lasting impression. Whether you're in the mood for something classic or adventurous, our chefs are dedicated to crafting dishes that delight your taste buds and leave you wanting more.
